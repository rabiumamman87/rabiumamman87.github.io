About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Iowa. My research focuses on artificial muscles, smart materials, and adaptive mechanical systems for soft robotics, flow control, and bio-inspired surfaces.

Selected Publications

  • Vacuum-driven artificial muscles exploiting snap-through instability for fast and efficient actuation.
  • Bioinspired active vortex generators for underwater flow control.
  • Fouling release mechanism of an octopus-inspired smart skin.
  • Deployable vortex generators for low Reynolds number applications powered by cephalopod-inspired artificial muscles.
